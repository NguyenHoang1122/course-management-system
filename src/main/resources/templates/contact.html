Our origins

Growing up in a small Vietnamese village, our founder had few educational opportunities — until they got access to a computer. Fueled by a dream to excel in technology, they used the internet to learn, eventually leading them to represent their country in international competitions.

After learning online changed their life, they partnered with industry experts to achieve a common goal: to make quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

How we started

From an early age, our founder knew learning was the key to unlocking opportunity. Our platform was born from the belief that knowledge should be shared, not gatekept.
